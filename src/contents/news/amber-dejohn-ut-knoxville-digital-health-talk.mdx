# Amber DeJohn invited to speak at UT Knoxville on digital health in later life

Amber DeJohn was recently invited to speak at the **University of Tennessee, Knoxville** about her work on digital health in later life.

The invited talk extended SDSC's visibility in conversations around aging, technology, and health, and created another opportunity to share Amber's research with public health scholars beyond Florida State University.

The photo from the visit includes Amber with AJ Wray, who organized the event, and Max Hartt, a Fulbright scholar currently spending his Fulbright at Vanderbilt.
