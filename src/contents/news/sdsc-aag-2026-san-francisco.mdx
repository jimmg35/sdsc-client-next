# SDSC at AAG 2026 in San Francisco

The Spatial Data Science Center (SDSC) demonstrated a strong presence at the **2026 American Association of Geographers (AAG) Annual Meeting**, held in San Francisco from **March 17-21, 2026**. Representing the cutting edge of spatial science, **3 faculty members and 7 graduate students** shared their latest research across mobility, public health, local spatial modeling, and causal inference.

Presentations from SDSC members included:

- **Weining Kan:** *Who Cancels Trips and Why? Quantitative Evidence on Post-Pandemic Mobility in Later Life*
- **Hanbin Wang:** *On Spatially Clustered Regression: An Empirical Comparison of Existing Methods*
- **Dr. Ziqi Li:** *The explicit fairness but implicit bias in mortgage lending in Florida*
- **Ju He:** *The Causal Effect of Distance on Spatial Outcomes*
- **Zhipeng Li:** *Why Place Matters: Revisiting Spatial Contextual Effects through Causal Inference*
- **Chen-Lun Kao:** *Uncovering Local Spatial Context via Multiscale Geographically Weighted Binomial Regression*
- **Dr. Amber DeJohn:** *Negotiating Mobility: Methodological Insights from Older Adults' Activity Diaries*
- **Dr. Mehak Sachdeva:** *Dynamic Social Encounters and the Shifting Landscape of Urban Political Polarization*
- **Stephen Liwur:** *After the flames and smoke, then what? Spatial analysis and heterogeneity modeling of bushfire effects on vegetation health and air quality in Ghana*
- **Md Abdul Fattah:** *Incorporating "Eligibility Constraints" in Spatial Access Modeling and Introducing Rational Agent Access Model (eRAAM)*

SDSC members were also recognized for excellence in research and service within AAG specialty groups:

- **Stephen Liwur** won the **Health Data Visualization Award** organized by the **Health and Medical Geography Specialty Group (HMGSG)**
- **Weining Kan** was elected **Graduate Student Board Member (2026-2028)** of the **Health and Medical Geography Specialty Group (HMGSG)** of the American Association of Geographers
- **Dr. Ziqi Li** was elected **Board Member (2026-2029)** of the **Spatial Analysis and Modelling (SAM) Specialty Group**

SDSC also hosted a social event on **March 18** at the **Press Club in San Francisco** together with the **FSU Department of Geography**. The gathering brought together more than 25 students, faculty, and alumni from SDSC and the department, along with **Dr. Taylor Oshan** (University of Maryland) and **Dr. Levi Wolf** (University of Bristol), both former advisees and collaborators of Center Director **Dr. A. Stewart Fotheringham**.
