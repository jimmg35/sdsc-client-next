# Mehak Sachdeva receives First Year Assistant Professor Grant for urban polarization research

Mehak Sachdeva, Assistant Professor in the Department of Urban and Regional Planning and an affiliate of the **Spatial Data Science Center (SDSC)**, has been awarded a **2025-2026 First Year Assistant Professor (FYAP) Grant** for her project, **"Placing Politics: A High-Resolution National Database for Analyzing Urban Polarization."**

The project will construct a longitudinal, individual-level voter file dataset covering **2019-2022**. This new data infrastructure will support research on political engagement and partisan segregation dynamics across the United States.

By building a high-resolution national database, the project strengthens SDSC's work in spatial social science and expands the center's capacity to study how political behavior varies across places, communities, and time.
