# Amber DeJohn to lead new national healthcare accessibility grant

Center Faculty Dr. Amber DeJohn (Principal Investigator) has been recently awarded the project **“National, Multidimensional Measures of Healthcare Accessibility for AD/ADRD Risk Factors,”** funded by the NIH-supported Gateway Exposome Coordinating Center (GECC). More to come.
